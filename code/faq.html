Frequently Asked Questions

What is this website about?
This website is designed to help users detect and mitigate bias in their datasets. Users can upload their dataset files, and the system will analyze the data for any potential bias. If bias is detected, it will automatically apply mitigation techniques and provide an updated version of the dataset along with an analysis report.
Is there any cost associated with using this service?
No, the service is completely free of cost. You can upload your datasets, get the analysis, and receive the updated dataset with bias mitigation without any charges.
What kind of datasets can I upload?
You can upload datasets in common formats like CSV, Excel, or JSON. The system is designed to handle most structured datasets, including those containing numerical, categorical, or mixed data types.
What types of bias can the system detect?
Our system can detect several types of bias, such as gender, racial, age, and socioeconomic biases. It can also identify issues related to class imbalance, data representation, and fairness in the dataset.
Will I get a report after the bias is mitigated?
Yes, after the bias detection and mitigation process, you will receive a detailed analysis report. The report will explain the biases found, the steps taken to mitigate them, and the impact of those changes on the dataset's fairness.
How can I download the updated dataset after bias mitigation?
Once the bias mitigation process is complete, you can download the updated dataset directly from the website. A download link will be provided along with the analysis report.
